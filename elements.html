What you need to get started

Some things you need to do to get ready to snowboard is get the right gear. You can rent to gear from stores near the summits or you can buy your own. The gear you will need is a helmet, a board, bindings for the board so you can connect yout feet to the board, snowboarding boots, gloves, and long snow pants and jackets.
